Gadies and Lentlemen, Welcome to Spot the Difference!

By Safwan Ahmed

Instructions

Use your cursor to hover over the images.

After you do so, the image will change.

Your job is to spot the difference using your sharp eye!

There are three levels: Easy, Medium, and lastly... "I want to rip my hair out" level.

Good luck! And here's the number for the National Mental Help Hotline in case you need it.

1-800-950-6264

Easy

You have 15 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.

Medium

You have 7 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.

I want to rip my hair out level

You have 5 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.
